Competition Rules & Guidelines:

  • The competition is open to all colleges (Inter-college event).
  • Each team must consist of 2 to 8 members.
  • All team members must belong to the same college.
  • Participants must carry their College ID cards on the event day.
  • Time limit: 5-10 minutes. Exceeding the time limit may result in deduction of marks.
  • Any dance style is allowed (Classical, Folk, Bollywood, Hip-hop, Contemporary, Fusion, etc.).
  • Teams must bring their music track in MP3 format on a pen drive and submit it 30 minutes prior to the event.
  • Use of props is allowed but must be declared during registration.
  • Use of fire, water, glass, or any hazardous materials on stage is strictly prohibited.
  • Objectionable, vulgar, or offensive content will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Teams must report at the venue 45 minutes before the event begins.

Additional Rules

  • A participant cannot be part of more than one team.
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to make changes if required.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
